#### 1780-08-01

rPassy 1st. Agt. 1780.  
The Bearer of this, Mr. appleton, is lately arrived from Boston. He is recommended to me as a young Gentleman of excellent character, &amp; as such, I beg leave to introduce him to your acquaintance and Civilities.  
With great Esteem, I am Dear Sir your most obedient &amp; most humble Servant.  
B. Franklin.  
M. Dumas.
